# Levrack.com Behaviors

## Scroll Behaviors
- Header does NOT change on scroll (not sticky, not fixed)
- No scroll-snap anywhere
- No Lenis or smooth scroll library
- No scroll-driven animations detected
- No parallax effects

## Click Behaviors
- Nav links with "3" suffix have dropdown submenus (Shop, About, Resources, Industries)
- CTA buttons navigate to product/category pages
- Carousel prev/next buttons cycle brand logos
- Carousel dot buttons jump to specific slide
- Popup form close button (X) dismisses the overlay
- Product cards link to individual product pages

## Hover Behaviors
- CTA buttons: transition 0.3s (likely color/bg change)
- Nav links: transition 0.4s ease-in-out
- Product cards: likely subtle hover effect

## Time-Driven Behaviors
- WhoUsesLevrack carousel auto-advances (brand logo slider)
- Hero video autoplays and loops

## Popup/Modal
- Bloom email signup popup appears on page load
- Has close button, form fields (First Name, Last Name, Email)
- CTA: "SOUNDS GOOD. I'M IN."

## Responsive Notes
- Mobile: hamburger menu replaces nav links
- Products stack vertically on mobile
- Workstations section loses horizontal scroll
- Footer columns stack vertically
